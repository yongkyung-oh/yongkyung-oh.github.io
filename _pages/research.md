### Research Interests

<div class="notice" markdown="1">

- _Physics-Informed Digital Twins for Complex Operations:_ Integrating physical knowledge with neural differential equations to build high-fidelity models of complex operation systems, with applications in predictive maintenance and automated quality control.
- _Causal Inference in Continuous-Time for Operational Analytics:_ Discovering causal structures in dynamic, irregular data to enable automated root-cause analysis, with a focus on explainable AI for fault diagnosis and intervention design in industrial processes.
- _Robust Decision-Making Under Uncertainty:_ Integrating predictive uncertainty from neural stochastic models into operational optimization frameworks and developing distributionally robust methods for operations planning and resource allocation.

</div>
